About Smartried

Who We Are

Bridging the Gap Between Academics & Industry

Smartried Training & Placement is a leading IT training institute dedicated to preparing students, fresh graduates, and working professionals for successful careers in the software industry.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world industry requirements through practical, job-oriented training programs.

We offer comprehensive training in high-demand technologies such as Python, Java, Data Engineering (Azure), DevOps, and Automation Testing. All programs are designed and delivered by experienced industry professionals, with a strong focus on hands-on learning and real-time project exposure.

Beyond training, we provide end-to-end placement support including resume building, mock interviews, and direct connections with hiring companies. Additionally, our IT outsourcing services enable businesses to access skilled professionals for development, testing, and support - while creating real-world opportunities for our trained candidates.

At Smartried, we are committed to helping individuals build confident, job-ready careers in the fast-growing IT industry.
