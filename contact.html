FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I Enroll now session?

Simply fill out the contact form above, call us, or send an email. Our team will guide you through the enrollment process and get you started.

Are the programs available online?

Yes, all our programs are available in both online and offline (classroom) modes. You can choose the mode that best suits your schedule and learning style.

What if I have no prior IT experience?

Most of our programs are designed for beginners. Our trainers will guide you step-by-step from the fundamentals. No prior IT experience is required for programs like Java Full Stack, Python Full Stack, and Automation Testing.

Is placement support available for all programs?

Yes, placement support is included with all our training programs. This includes resume building, mock interviews, career counseling, and direct referrals to our hiring partners.

How are the batch sizes structured?

We intentionally keep our batch sizes small - typically a maximum of 15–20 students - to ensure every learner receives personalized attention and quality guidance throughout the program.
